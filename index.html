About

I built this after watching my non-Chinese partner struggle for a year in China. From dead Western map apps to Chinese-only ticketing systems and unexpected 'Locals Only' hotel rejections, the digital wall is a real barrier to exploring this incredible country.

Formscape Travel isn't just a map; it's a bridge. It takes the highly accurate, hyper-local data that Chinese citizens use every day and wraps it in an English-first, VPN-free command center. It is the exact toolset I built to ensure you spend your vacation actually discovering the city, not just trying to survive it.
